What is an ISBN?

The International Standard Book Number (ISBN) is a unique identifier assigned to books and other publications, making it easier for libraries, bookstores, and distributors to find and order your book.

ISBNs are essential for both print and digital books if you want your work to be officially listed and distributed globally.
