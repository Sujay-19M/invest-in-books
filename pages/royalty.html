How We Calculate Your Royalties

Your royalty earnings depend on factors such as book price, sales channel, and region. Here’s an example of how it works:

  • 💰 Sale Price: ₹300
  • 💰 Platform Fees (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.): ₹60
  • 💰 Printing & Distribution Cost: ₹80
  • 💰 Net Royalty: ₹160 per sale

We ensure you receive maximum possible earnings by optimizing pricing and choosing cost-effective distribution partners.

Royalty Payment Schedule

Royalties are processed and paid to authors as per the following schedule:

  • 📅 Monthly Payouts (for sales above ₹500)
  • 📅 Quarterly Payouts (for sales below ₹500)
